Welcome to Takachain

Takachain is a revolutionary decentralized application (DApp) built on the Avalanche testnet, designed to incentivize and streamline plastic waste collection and recycling. Our mission is to connect individuals who collect plastic waste ("Pickers"), or even citizens like you simply relaxing at home, with recycling companies, ensuring transparent and efficient payment for their valuable environmental efforts.

By leveraging blockchain technology, Takachain provides a secure, immutable, and transparent platform for tracking plastic deposits and managing payments. This fosters trust and encourages greater participation in recycling initiatives, contributing to a cleaner, greener planet.

Join us in making a difference, one plastic bottle at a time!

About Avalanche

What is Avalanche? Avalanche is a decentralized, open source, Layer 1 blockchain platform, designed to support fast, scalable, and secure decentralized applications (dApps) and smart contracts.

Avalanche’s architecture features three core blockchains. The X-Chain (Exchange Chain), C-Chain (Contract Chain), and P-Chain (Platform Chain), working together to respectively manage assets (sending of funds), smart contracts, and network coordination, making it a flexible competitor to platforms like Ethereum, Bitcoin, Base, Starknet, Tezos, Solana, Terra and Hyperledger Fabric.

Known for its PoW (Proof of Work) Avalanche consensus algorithm, it blends classical as well as Nakamoto consensus methods to deliver high transaction speeds (throughput), low delays (latency), and strong decentralization, processing thousands of transactions per second with near instant finality.

In order to use Avalanche, one uses a wallet called Core Wallet, Avalanche’s native wallet. Designed for speed and safety, it gives you a powerful multichain experience, letting you send and receive assets, connect to decentralized apps, and explore the Avalanche ecosystem without friction. With Core Wallet, you’re ready to dive into Web3 on Avalanche with confidence.

Setting Up Core Wallet

Step 1: Install Core Extension

  1. Open Google Chrome or any Chromium based browser (Brave, Edge, Opera).
  2. Go to the Core Chrome Web Store page.
  3. Click Add to Chrome and confirm the installation.
  4. Once installed, you’ll see the Core icon in your browser’s extensions bar.

Step 2: Create a Wallet

  1. Click the Core icon in your browser.
  2. Select Create New Wallet.
  3. Set a strong password and confirm it.
  4. Write down your Secret Recovery Phrase and store it securely, do not share it with anyone.
  5. Confirm the recovery phrase to finish creating your wallet.

Step 3: Connect Core Wallet to Avalanche Fuji Testnet

Takachain is currently live on the Avalanche Fuji Testnet for testing.

  1. Open Core Wallet.
  2. Click the network dropdown (top of the wallet window).
  3. Select Fuji (Testnet) from the list.

Step 4: Fund Your Wallet with Test Tokens

  1. Copy your Core wallet address (starts with 0x...).
  2. Visit the Avalanche Faucet.
  3. Paste your address and request Fuji Test AVAX.
  4. Wait a few seconds — your balance should update in Core Wallet.

Step 5: Connect Core Wallet to Takachain

  1. Open the Takachain DApp/Website in your browser.
  2. Click Connect Wallet.
  3. Choose Core and approve the connection in the wallet popup.
  4. You’re ready to start using Takachain! 🎉

Security Tips:

  • Never share your Secret Recovery Phrase with anyone.
  • Only use the official Core extension from the Chrome Web Store.

About Takachain

Takachain was founded on the principle that technology can be a powerful tool for social and environmental good. Our team is passionate about addressing the global plastic waste crisis and empowering communities.

Our Mission
